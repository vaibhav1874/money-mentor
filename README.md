# 💰 MoneyMitra AI  
### Your AI-Powered Financial Mentor 🚀  

MoneyMitra AI is a modern, AI-driven personal finance platform designed to transform how individuals manage, understand, and grow their money.  

Unlike traditional apps that only track expenses, **MoneyMitra AI acts like a real financial advisor** — helping users make smarter decisions through intelligent insights, personalized recommendations, and future planning.

---

## 🌟 Project Vision  

> **"Bridging the Gap Between Data and Decisions."**

Most financial tools tell you *what happened*.  
MoneyMitra AI tells you:
- **Why it happened**
- **What it means**
- **What you should do next**

---

## 🚀 Key Features  

### 💯 Money Health Score  
A dynamic financial wellness score (0–100) based on:
- Emergency preparedness  
- Insurance coverage  
- Investment diversification  
- Debt health  
- Tax efficiency  
- Retirement readiness  

👉 Gives users an instant snapshot of their financial health.

---

### 🤖 AI-Powered Financial Insights  
- Detects spending patterns  
- Flags unusual behavior  
- Suggests actionable improvements  

Examples:
- “Dining expenses increased by 45%”  
- “You can save ₹10,000/year by optimizing subscriptions”  

---

### 💬 Conversational AI (MoneyMitra Chat)  
Ask real questions like:
- “Can I afford a ₹50,000 trip?”  
- “Where should I invest ₹10k?”  

👉 Get personalized, context-aware answers powered by AI.

---

### 🎯 Goal Planner  
Plan financial goals like:
- Buying a house 🏠  
- Travel ✈️  
- Emergency fund  

👉 AI calculates:
- Required SIP  
- Timeline  
- Investment strategy  

---

### 🏦 Bank Statement Analysis  
- Upload CSV/PDF bank statements  
- Auto-categorize transactions  
- Generate insights instantly  

---

## 🧠 Advanced AI Modules  

### 🔥 FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) Planner  
- Builds month-by-month financial roadmap  
- Suggests SIP amounts  
- Tracks asset allocation  
- Identifies insurance gaps  
- Plans emergency funds  

---

### 🎉 Life Event Financial Advisor  
Handles major life events:
- Marriage 💍  
- Bonus 💰  
- New baby 👶  

👉 Provides customized financial strategies based on:
- Income  
- Risk profile  
- Tax bracket  

---

### 💸 Tax Wizard  
- Upload Form 16 or salary details  
- Compare old vs new tax regime  
- Suggest deductions & investments  

👉 Optimizes tax savings intelligently.

---

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Couple’s Money Planner  
- Joint financial planning tool  
- Optimizes:
  - HRA claims  
  - NPS contributions  
  - SIP distribution  
  - Insurance strategies  

👉 Provides combined net worth tracking.

---

### 📊 Mutual Fund Portfolio X-Ray  
- Upload CAMS / KFintech statement  
- Get:
  - Portfolio breakdown  
  - XIRR calculation  
  - Overlap analysis  
  - Expense ratio insights  
  - AI-based rebalancing suggestions
